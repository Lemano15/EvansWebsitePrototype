Executive Board 2020

Patrick McCormick

President

Pat McCormack is the new President of the Illinois Evans House. Pat is from West Chicago and caddied at Royal Fox Country Club. He is majoring in Econometrics and Quantitative Economics, with a minor in Political Science. When asked what he is most excited for this year as President he said he’s excited for collaborating with scholars to make the house the best it can be!

The University of Illinois Evans Scholars

Who We Are

The Illinois Chapter is the largest chapter of 18 in the Evans Scholarship Foundation. Established in 1951, it is the second chapter to be organized by the Wester Golf Association, with Northwestern University being the first. Currently, there are 138 active scholars with over 1,300 alumni.

What We Do

The Illinois Evans Scholars are always engaging in academic, athletic, and volunteer extracurriculars across campus. Throughout the year, we host various events such as blood drives and professional development workshops, volunteer at soup kitchens and food distributing centers, and also administer fundraisers for various causes such as breast cancer.
